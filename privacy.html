Privacy Policy

YOUR DATA IS SAFE WITH US

1. Data Protection & Safety

At IntoThePeaks, we prioritize the safety of your personal information. All customer data collected during the booking process is stored in encrypted databases. We ensure that your information is used solely for trek coordination and permit processing with local authorities.

No Data Sharing Policy

We strictly guarantee that your Phone Number and personal details will never be shared, sold, or rented to any third-party marketing agencies.

2. Payment Security

Your financial security is our top concern. We use industry-standard security protocols to handle transactions:

  • SSL Encryption: All payment sessions are protected via Secure Socket Layer technology.
  • Secure Gateways: We only use verified payment partners (UPI, Razorpay, or Direct Bank Transfer).
  • No Card Storage: IntoThePeaks does not store your Credit/Debit card or Bank password details on our servers.

3. Information We Collect

To provide a smooth Himalayan experience, we collect:

  • Basic Contact (Name, Phone, Email)
  • Government ID (For Forest/Police Permits)
  • Emergency Contact & Medical Info (For your safety)

4. Our Commitment

We only contact you for trek-related updates, itinerary shares, or safety instructions. We hate spam as much as you do, so you will only receive essential communication from our official team.
